Clear candidates for merging:

paper_urls + paper_links — Both store URLs associated with a paper. paper_urls tracks discovery sources, paper_links tracks extracted links (PDF, SSRN, etc.). These could be one table with a source or link_type discriminator. Right now it's confusing which one to use.

research_fields / researcher_fields vs jel_codes / researcher_jel_codes — Two parallel classification systems for the same researchers. research_fields is a custom 12-item taxonomy; JEL codes are the economics standard. You could drop the custom fields entirely and just use JEL codes, eliminating 2 tables.
